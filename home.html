About

Hello, I'm Malin, a multi-disciplinary designer originally from Lusaka, Zambia, and now based in Paris. I'm currently in my final semester pursuing a bachelors degree in Art, Media, and Technology at Parsons School of Design in Paris, where I've been able to hone my skills in programming and front-end development. With a passion for creating visually stunning, user-friendly designs, I've gained hands-on experience in front-end development, UI design, animation, and graphic design.
